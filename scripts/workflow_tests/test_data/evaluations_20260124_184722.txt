Primary weakness: The final section on modern relevance feels slightly obligatory and less technically grounded than the preceding material, transitioning from rigorous uncertainty-acknowledgment to somewhat generic warming warnings

=== FINDING ===
Primary strength: Strong technical depth with clear explanations of mechanisms—the heat transfer physics section explaining prolonged duration is particularly well-integrated and illuminating
Primary weakness: The framing as a 'striking finding' (cascade/tipping points) somewhat oversells what is actually a nuanced story of persistent uncertainty; the opening implies we've discovered something decisive when the body reveals we haven't
Scores: hook=4, momentum=4, payoff=4, tone=4, complexity=4, fit=3

=== CONTRARIAN ===
Primary strength: The epistemological framing about what ancient events can and cannot tell us is genuinely thoughtful and the essay does excellent work exposing methodological limitations in proxy records
Primary weakness: The contrarian frame is somewhat forced—the 'comfortable story' it pushes against (that we understand the PETM) is already contested within the scientific community; this isn't contrarian so much as accurate representation of existing scientific debate
Scores: hook=4, momentum=4, payoff=4, tone=4, complexity=5, fit=3
